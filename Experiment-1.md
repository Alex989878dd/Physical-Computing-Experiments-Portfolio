# Experiment 1 - MicroServo SG90 & Photo resistor

## Description
My goal was to create a circuit where photo resistor would move MiscroServo arm depending on amount of light in the room. The main question I’m was trying to answer is: how can light levels (environment input) control physical movement (feedback)? I broke down this task into 2 steps: 1) controlling motor arm with code; 2) adding a photoresistor to the circuit and making the motor’s behaviour dependent on the light level.

---

## Components
* Arduino
* USB – B Power Cable
* Breadboard
* Jumper Cables
* Servo Motor with attachments
* Photoresistor
